Requires NDA. Takes 2–4 weeks to build proof environment. Best for mid-stage validation.
POC Option 3 — Enterprise POC (paid)
Full paid engagement
~3 months. Used for complex enterprise deals where full feasibility must be proven before contract.

Planhat consolidates CRM, service tools, telemetry, and vendor portals into one Customer 360. Every account, every vendor, one view.
2. Action
Teams operate the full lifecycle — workflows, automations, tasks — from the same platform. No context-switching between tools.
3. Growth
Churn prevention, improved renewals, expansion triggers, and team alignment all driving NRR upward.

Reference something specific — their role, company, city, or vertical signal
Always end with a soft question — not a hard pitch
Follow-up nudges should be one-liners — no re-pitching, just re-surfacing
Personalize the Cisco angle only if they are a verified Cisco partner

Expansion Playbook
Land-and-expand model, lifecycle motion, and real account case studies
Land size
~20%
of total deal potential. Start small, grow as vendors are added.
Growth driver
Each new vendor = more data, more users, more features, more ARR
Tagline
"All customers. One control center."
Lifecycle motion
Land
One vendor (typically Cisco). ~20% of total deal potential. Fast implementation, quick win.
→ Go-live in weeks
Adopt
Team builds habits in Planhat. Health scores, renewal workflows, and automations embedded in daily operations.
→ Stickiness achieved
Expand
Add vendors (Microsoft, Palo Alto, etc.), more users, more custom objects. Each expansion = new ARR.
→ Deal grows 2–5×
Renew
Renewal is easy because Planhat is embedded. Expansion opportunities surface naturally through health data.
→ NRR > 120%
